of players surveyed have played in the wrong footwear — and did it anyway, because missing the game felt worse than the risk of injury.

73%
rank injury prevention as their #1 priority — above cost, above performance. And yet 53% play in gear that threatens exactly that.

A note on method. These numbers come from 55 active players across recreational and competitive levels, plus three in-depth interviews with players who had experienced real, documented injuries. We weren't looking for edge cases. We kept finding the same story.

Act IV · The System

ReCleat doesn't sell cleats.
It closes every gap the market left open.

🔒

Modular Lock System

Visual + tactile confirmation on every swap. Green indicator. Audible click. No ambiguity about whether the outsole is secure.

⚡

<10-Second Swap

Sideline-ready. No tools, no rehearsal, no pre-game decision anxiety. The right outsole for the surface, in under ten seconds.

💰

$49 Outsole vs $190 Replacement

The platform model inverts the economics. Players pay for adaptability, not for the failure of a system that forces full replacements.
